Our Story

Founded in 2025, Xize was created by a single developer with a simple mission: to empower teams and individuals to achieve more by simplifying their work. Xize is a product of NXH, the same way Facebook is a product of Meta. We believe that productivity should be effortless and that the right tools can make all the difference. Our platform is built on the principles of efficiency, collaboration, and innovation, helping you to not just manage tasks, but to conquer your goals.

From a single idea, Xize has grown into a dedicated project committed to building the future of task management. We are constantly listening to our users and evolving our platform to meet the dynamic needs of the modern workplace.
